How it works

Set up in 5 minutes,
works forever.

Three steps and you're done. No more checking five apps every Sunday night.

1

Add your family members

Create a profile for each kid with a name and color. They'll appear in every event title so you can see at a glance whose event it is.

2

Connect your sports apps

Paste the iCal link from each app — TeamSnap, GameChanger, PlayMetrics, and more. SportsCal fetches and combines them automatically.

3

Subscribe once, done forever

Copy your personal feed URL and subscribe in Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, or Outlook. It stays live and updates automatically.
