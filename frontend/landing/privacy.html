Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how 5K Consulting LLP ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use SportsCal ("Service"). We take your privacy seriously — especially since SportsCal deals with your family's schedule.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash — we never store your actual password).

Calendar data: We collect the iCal feed URLs you provide and the event data retrieved from those feeds (event titles, times, locations, and descriptions). This data is stored to power your unified calendar feed.

Family member information: Names and color preferences you enter for family members.

Payment information: If you subscribe to Premium, your payment is processed by Stripe. We store only a Stripe customer ID — we never see or store your full credit card number.

Usage data: Basic server logs including IP addresses, request timestamps, and error information for debugging purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the Service — aggregating your sports calendars and generating your personal iCal feed
  • To send transactional emails — welcome emails, password resets, and weekly digest emails (if enabled)
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To troubleshoot issues and improve the Service
  • To communicate important changes to the Service or these policies

We do not use your calendar data for advertising, we do not sell your data to third parties, and we do not share your personal information with anyone except as described below.

3. Information Sharing

We share information only with the following service providers who help us operate the Service:

  • Railway — cloud hosting for our servers and database
  • Resend — email delivery service
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Twilio — SMS delivery for ride request notifications (Premium subscribers only)
  • Sentry — error monitoring and crash reporting; reports do not include your account identifiers

Each of these providers has their own privacy policy and is contractually required to protect your data. We do not share your data with any other third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Calendar event data is refreshed regularly and old events are pruned automatically. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

5. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS), hashed passwords, and access controls. However, no system is perfectly secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at [email protected].

6. Children's Privacy

SportsCal is designed for use by parents and guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Family member profiles (for kids) contain only a name and color preference — no contact information or personal details.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data (directly in the SportsCal mobile app via Settings → Delete account, or through your web account settings)
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Opt out of marketing emails (transactional emails cannot be disabled)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at [email protected].

8. Cookies

SportsCal does not use tracking cookies. To keep you signed in we store your authentication token in your browser's localStorage on the web, or the iOS Keychain in our mobile app. No advertising cookies, no third-party trackers.

9. SMS Messaging

SportsCal's Ride Coordination feature allows Premium subscribers to send SMS pickup and dropoff requests to contacts (family members, coaches, neighbors) they have added to their account.

How consent is collected. When a SportsCal user adds a contact to their account and assigns that contact to a pickup or dropoff for an event, SportsCal sends an SMS from a Twilio number on the user's behalf. By adding a phone number to their contacts and assigning it to an event, the SportsCal user represents that they have obtained the contact's consent to receive these messages. Users are prompted to confirm this consent before the first message is sent to a given contact.

Message content. Messages are limited to pickup/dropoff coordination for specific events (example: "Can you pick up Levi from Tigard HS on Thursday at 4pm? Reply YES or NO."). SportsCal does not send marketing, promotional, or other unsolicited messages to contacts.

Message frequency. Message frequency varies based on the SportsCal user's activity, typically ranging from zero to a few messages per week per contact.

Opt-out. Recipients may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for support. Once a recipient opts out, SportsCal will not send further messages to that number unless the recipient later opts back in.

Message and data rates. Message and data rates may apply based on the recipient's mobile carrier plan. SportsCal does not charge recipients for these messages.

Phone number handling. Mobile phone numbers are collected solely for the purpose of sending ride coordination messages initiated by the SportsCal account holder. Mobile phone numbers and SMS consent are not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Numbers are shared only with Twilio, our SMS delivery provider, as necessary to deliver messages.
